The Purpose:

Many student athletes find themselves letting their sport take control of their life. Fewer than 2% of NCAA athletes go on to play professionally, which in this case, is understandable for a student to priotize athletics. However, the other 98% of the NCAA student athlete population will soon live a life without their sport. Outlet provides a list of resources from the university customized for the user's hobbies and interests.
